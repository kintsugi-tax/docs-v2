## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Data">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Connect Existing System">
        Click **Connect** in the **Data Sources** section to integrate with your current platform.
        
        <Tabs>
          <Tab title="Direct Integration">
            Kintsugi offers dozens of prebuilt integrations to various shopping carts, ERP's, and more.
          </Tab>
          <Tab title="Custom Integration">
            Use our REST API or SDK's to build custom integrations with any platform.
          </Tab>
          <Tab title="Manual Upload">
            Kintsugi has a CSV template that's simple to use, allowing you to manually import your data.
          </Tab>
        </Tabs>
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Import Historical Data">
        Have CSV files with historical transactions? Use our **File Upload** functionality to import them.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Your Business">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Physical Presence">
        Specify jurisdictions where you have a physical presence:
        
        - **Offices or warehouses**
        - **Remote employees**
        - **Inventory storage**
        - **Sales representatives**
        
        <Tip>
          Don't worry if you're unsure - our system will help you identify nexus requirements based on your business activities.
        </Tip>
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Organization Details">
        Add your business information so we can register and file in the right jurisdictions:
        
        - Business name and address
        - Tax ID numbers
        - Business type and industry
        - Contact information
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Banking Information">
        Provide bank details for tax remittance:
        
        - Bank account for tax payments
        - ACH information
        - Payment preferences
        
        <Warning>
          This information is encrypted and stored securely. We only use it for tax remittance purposes.
        </Warning>
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Products & Tax Rules">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Product Classification">
        Approve your products so we can apply accurate tax rates:
        
        <Tabs>
          <Tab title="Bulk Approval">
            Use our **bulk approve** functionality to approve all products at once.
          </Tab>
          <Tab title="Kintsugi Intelligence">
            Let our AI automatically classify your products using **Kintsugi Intelligence**.
          </Tab>
          <Tab title="Manual Review">
            Review and approve products individually for maximum control.
          </Tab>
        </Tabs>
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Address Validation">
        Ensure all addresses are valid and complete:
        
        - Check the **Tasks** section for invalid addresses
        - Use **Kintsugi Intelligence** to automatically add missing addresses
        - Review and correct any flagged addresses
        
        <Check>
          Valid addresses are crucial for accurate tax calculations!
        </Check>
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review & Launch">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Nexus Review">
        Check your nexus exposure:
        
        - Navigate to the **Nexus** section
        - Review the exposure map on your dashboard
        - Understand your filing obligations
        
        <Info>
          Our system continuously monitors your nexus status and alerts you to new requirements.
        </Info>
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Exemption Management">
        Set up tax exemptions:
        
        - Navigate to the **Exemptions** section
        - Configure customer exemptions
        - Set up product exemptions
        - Manage exemption certificates
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Registration Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register in New Jurisdictions">
    Click **Register** on the Nexus page to register in jurisdictions where you have nexus.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Import Existing Registrations">
    If you're already registered somewhere, import your existing registrations so we can generate filings for you.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="File and Remit">
    Check your filings on the **Filings** page and click **Approve** to file and remit taxes in each jurisdiction.
  </Step>
</Steps>
